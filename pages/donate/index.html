Support Collab Lab!

The Collab Lab is volunteer-driven and free for participants. We intentionally and actively work to overcome barriers to opportunities for women and other under-represented minorities in tech.

We make use of free/freemium tools (i.e. Airtable, Firebase, GitHub, Google Docs, Netlify, Slack, Notion, and Zoom) as much as possible. We would love to have the ability to use upgraded versions of some of these tools to provide a better experience for our volunteers and participants.
